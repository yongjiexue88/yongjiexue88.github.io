# What I want to remember this year

Not goals. Just a few things I'd like to keep close while the year unfolds.

- The version of me that is patient is also the version that is happiest.
- It is okay to have a small life on purpose.
- Saying *I don't know* in public is a kind of generosity.
- Most of my regrets are about things I rushed.

> A year is too long to plan. A morning is enough to begin.

That's all for today.

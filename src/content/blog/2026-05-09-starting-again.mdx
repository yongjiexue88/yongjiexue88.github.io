# Starting Again

I used to treat this website like a technical profile. It had projects, skills, notes, and the kind of information that makes sense when a website is mainly trying to explain what I can build.

But I want this place to become something slower.

I want to write diaries here. I want to leave small records of what I am thinking about, what I am avoiding, what I am learning, and what I notice when I am not trying to sound polished.

## Why change it?

- Technical notes can live somewhere else.
- A personal website does not always need to perform.
- Writing is easier to continue when the space already feels honest.

## What I want this site to hold

This blog can become a personal archive: reflections, life updates, language practice, book notes, and occasional thoughts about technology when they connect to real life.

> The goal is not to publish perfect essays. The goal is to remember what I was paying attention to.

# Late spring in Austin

The mornings are loud now. Birds, then traffic, then the AC unit downstairs deciding whether it is summer yet.

I left the windows open last night for the first time. The room smelled like wet pavement and something green I couldn't name.

## What I noticed

- The jacaranda outside is finally honest about being purple.
- Strangers walk slower when it is warm but not too warm.
- I forgot how much I like sleeping with a thin blanket.

> Texas has two seasons of mercy. This is one of them.

I want to remember the sound of windows being open. That, more than anything, is what makes a place feel like it is mine.

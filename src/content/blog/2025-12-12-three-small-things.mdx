# Three small things from December

I am bad at remembering whole years. I am better at remembering small things. So here are three from this month, before they slip.

- The morning the heater stopped working and we drank coffee in jackets like we were camping.
- A short phone call with my mother where neither of us said anything important and that was the point.
- The smell of orange peel on my hands after dinner. I could still find it the next morning.

That's it. That's the whole entry.

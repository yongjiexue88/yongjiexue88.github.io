# Notes from a quiet weekend

A weekend with no plans, on purpose.

- Made congee on Saturday morning. It was the kind of cold where the kitchen window fogged up and I let it.
- Re-read the first chapter of a book I half-finished last year. It was better the second time, which surprised me.
- Walked to the post office and noticed how everyone in line was patient. Maybe it was the weather. Maybe it was a Sunday thing.

That was the entire weekend. I don't think I lost anything by doing less.
